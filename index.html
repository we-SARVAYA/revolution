About

3 DAYS | 20+ EXPERTS

UNLIMITED LEARNING

KB Revolution 2025 is a premier three-day conference organized by Kitchen & Bath Global Partners (KABS), bringing together industry leaders, innovators, and professionals to explore the future of the kitchen and bath industry. With a strong focus on AI-powered design, business transformation, and hands-on learning, the event is designed to equip you with the tools and insights needed to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving market.

From inspiring keynotes and interactive workshops to cutting-edge technology showcases and unmatched networking opportunities, KB Revolution 2025 is where innovation meets opportunity. Whether you’re a designer, business owner, manufacturer, or tech enthusiast, this is the ultimate platform to learn, connect, and grow.

Event Venue

Explore the K&B Revolution Conference 2025 Venue and Location Details

The K&B Revolution Conference 2025 will be hosted at the National Conference Center in Leesburg, Virginia, a premier venue known for its modern facilities and seamless event experience. Nestled in a serene and well-connected location, the center offers spacious meeting halls, state-of-the-art technology, and comfortable accommodations, ensuring an inspiring and productive environment for all attendees.

But that’s not all - get ready for an unforgettable experience beyond the sessions! We’ve planned a special barbecue night packed with delicious food, lively conversations, and endless networking opportunities. Enjoy great music, interactive activities, and plenty of chances to connect with fellow industry leaders in a relaxed and fun atmosphere. It’s not just a conference - it’s a celebration of innovation, collaboration, and community!
